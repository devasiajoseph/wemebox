Our Business

Way Forward

Palathra Infra Developers Pvt. Ltd is an ISO 9001:2015 certified and a leading PWD ‘A’ class contractor based in Kottayam Kerala, India. Known for producing high quality deliverables, we undertake Industrial and Commercial civil construction projects all over the state. With over 45 years of experience in the market, we have established ourselves as the go-to brand for Kerala Public Work Department (PWD), irrigation and mobile tower works in Kerala.

Our unfaltering quality of service and customer-loyalty has rendered us an award-winning organization with several accolades to our name. High profile clients like, Kochi Metro Rail Limited, Kerala Motor Vehicle Department, JCI-Infrastructure Business Projects, Huawei Tele communication (Best TSP), etc have presented us with various awards as a token of appreciation for our diligent performances. Timely completion award from Bureaucrats besides Construction Committee are also part of our accomplishment.

At Palathra, we strive to push our limits daily as part of our quest to grow and expand our footsteps in all the directions. Our latest venture to undertake industrial and commercial projects of various types is a testimony to the diversification that we are welcoming to achieve our target of being a market leader. The categories of projects we can handle with expert ease include (but are not limited to) Land Development, Road Works (bituminous, concrete and interlock paving blocks), Commercial Buildings, Bridges, Drainage Works, Compound Wall Construction, Factory Yards, Parking Area, Interlock tiles, etc among many others.

An engineering team that is both qualified and experienced, a wide range of the up to date machineries and equipment, and a professional technical and quality team to support the execution. This exceptional combination gives us an upper hand to deliver a high quality product within the allotted time frame and budget.

Our ultimate mission is to improve the standard of living all across the region and contribute to the creation of a stronger nation. Teamwork, top quality service, meeting deadlines and client satisfaction form our core values which take precedence over any other objective. This is what defines our true value and makes us amongst the most dependable and best in the industry.
